background

These days, everyone seems to be on social media. You can connect with your friends, family, and even develop meaningful relationships with people that you may never actually meet face to face. Social Media platforms now offer everything we need to connect with one another (and more) for free...to everyone throughout the world. This unprecedented human network has affected our world in ways never thought possible, but are there drawbacks to this level of connectivity? Through videos, statistics, interviews, and a rolling conversation, we seek to examine the concept of Social Media Addiction.

interviews

Social Media Addiction can affect anyone. We interviewed real people to find out their feelings on social media addiction, as well as their own social media usage habits.

daily social media use

Social Media usage has become a daily habit for a majority of people worldwide, and daily usage rates have steadily risen over the past 15 years (and continue to rise). Is this constant increase just a symptom of how dependent we are on technology in modern times? Or is it a sign of a growing social media addiction worldwide?

learn more and share your thoughts

Social Media Addiction is an important topic in today's society. Recently, there have been many stories published about excessive social media use and its affect on emotions. We are also now seeing the maturation of the first generation that has lived with the Internet all their lives. Social Media has become undeniably tied into our day to day lives, but where will it end? Will we see the usage trends continue to climb? And if so, what affect will all that time spent on social media have on us as people, on our society, and on our abilities to interact with one another.
